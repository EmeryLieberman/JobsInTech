Marketing is the process of getting potential customers interested in products. Marketers analyze the relationships between the market and customer behaviours. All companies use marketing to grow their companies. Marketing can increase brand awareness, so that customers know that companies exist. Marketing is one of the key parts of any company, because if you want to make money you need to make sure people know you exist. Another main part of marketing is that it helps brands to be easily recognized by their logo.

There is a main marketing strategy that includes the essential elements of marketing. These elements are product, price, place, and promotion. There are also many types of marketing, such as email marketing, direct marketing, performance marketing, digital marketing, and many more. Additionally to these elements, marketing uses market research and analysis to effectively track performance. Because the purpose of marketing is the help businesses grow efficiently, marketing is very important to businesses.
