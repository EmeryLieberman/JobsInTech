Jobs In Technology

Technology is at the core of the modern world. Everything from devices like phones, TVs, and computers to the assembly lines and robots that manufacture the majority of the products we're accustomed to. There are millions of people behind the luxury that is technolgy we know today, and the majority not in ways you would expect. The American tech industry is worth billions. In 2020, the United States tech sector employed roughly 5.2 million workers, with software developers and software QA the largest occupation with 1.5 million workers. By 2030, the number of employed U.S. tech workers is forecasted to reach almost 6 million.

Globally, there is a shortage of millions tech workers. The US requires nearly 500,000, and need is growing by more than 100,000 per year. Yet, US colleges are only graduating about 70,000 a year. The demand will keep increasing, yet the amount of graduates stays consistent. When there's not enough supply, but demand keeps increasing, the salaries for tech workers have been on a near-constant climb for years.
